I

About Céleste

Céleste Vương is a Vietnamese-American voice actress, singer and writer with a voice made for the space between sweet and shadowy. Whether she's voicing a bubbly best friend or a centuries-old vampire with a soft spot, she brings warmth, range and a little mischief to every take.

She works from a home studio and has lent her voice to 84+ collaborative projects in the indie voice-acting community, always game for characters that live a little in the dark.

If you need a voice that can be both the girl-next-door and the thing at the window, Céleste would love to read for you.

"The sweetest voices always have a little bite." she/her, 21+
